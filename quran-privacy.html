Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Al-Qur'an, we greatly value and respect the privacy of our users. Our goal is to provide an excellent user experience while minimizing the collection of personal information.

Information Collection

Al-Qur'an does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not request information such as email addresses, and there is no requirement for users to create an account.

Log Data

While using our app, we may collect anonymous log data to help us understand how the service is being used. This data may include your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, device type, operating system version, the pages you visit within our app, the date and time of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other related statistics. This data is collected anonymously and does not include any personally identifiable information.

Use of Data

The anonymous log data we collect is used solely to analyze and improve the performance and user experience of our app. This data is not shared or used to identify individual users.

Use of Cookies

Al-Qur'an may use cookies to enhance the user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device while using our app. You have the option to manage or disable cookies through your device settings. The data collected through cookies does not contain any personally identifiable information.

Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of any data we collect. As Al-Qur'an does not collect personal information, we aim to maintain the highest standards in protecting user privacy and securing the data we process.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If any changes are made to this privacy policy, the updated version will be prominently posted within the app and/or on our website. We encourage users to regularly review the privacy policy to stay informed about how we are safeguarding your privacy.
